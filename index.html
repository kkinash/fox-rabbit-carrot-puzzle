Help Farmer Transport His Friends Across the River!

Find a way to transport all characters across the river without any of them getting eaten.

The boat is too small

The Farmer can only take one character with him on the boat at a time, so he must make multiple trips to transport all three characters.

Leave them alone very carefully

The Farmer can never leave the Fox and the Rabbit (or the Rabbit and the Carrot) together on one side of the river without supervision.

How to win?

In order to win, all characters must be on the other side of the river with the farmer
